What is vigorish?

The vigorish is how sportsbooks make money. Let’s assume balanced (the same amount of) action on both sides of a sporting event. The book has two bets, one for each team. Each bettor wants to win $100, so they have both bet $110. Two bets of $110 means the book has taken in $220. Whichever side wins the game, the book will pay out the original $110 bet, plus $100 in winnings, for a total of $210 paid out. The book took in $220 and paid out $210. That extra $10 is the profit for the book. It is also known as the Juice, or the Vigorish (Vig).
