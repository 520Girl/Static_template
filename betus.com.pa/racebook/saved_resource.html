PPs
A compilation in Daily Racing Form of a horse's record, including all pertinent data, as a basis for handicapping.
SPEED
Speed average from the last 5 races
DATE
The date when the previous races from a particular horse were carried out
RACE TYPE
Also known as RACE CLASSES. There are four primary horse racing classes: claiming races, maiden races, allowance races, and stakes races.
DISTANCE
The race distance is the total length over which the race will be run
SURFACE
The track surface of a horse racing track refers to the material of which the track is made. There are three types of track surfaces used in modern horse racing. These are: dirt, turf, and Artificial or Synthetic
TRACK
The track surface of a horse racing track prior to a horse race or race meet. It is determined by the amount of moisture in the ground and is assessed by an official steward on the day of the race
MED (Medication)
Refers to the medication list published by the track and Daily Racing Form (when provided by track officials) showing which horses have been treated with phenylbutazone and/or furosemide.
WT(Weight)
Fixed scale of weights to be carried by horses according to age, sex, the distance of the race, and season of the year.
TIMED
Duration of a specific race
POSITION
Finishing order of the horse in a particular race
RUNNER STYLE
It refers to the running styles of the horses, These are: Closer, Presser, Stalker, and Speed
